About the Cause

What we are about?

Our goal is to grow awareness of the endangerment of such a beautiful species and to support a cause that is very close to our hearts. We believe that with the support of our community and with the help of Ivory Ella, we can preserve the species and ensure that they can peacefully reside in their habitats.

What is Ivory Ella? What is their goal?

Ivory Ella is a for-profit clothing store organized and focused on the wildlife conservation of elephants. Their brand sells clothing and accessories, such as sweatshirts, sweatpants, and t-shirts. A portion of their proceeds are donated to nonprofit organizations who share their same goal of preserving the elephant population and their habitats.

Ivory Ella's goal is to make a difference and save them from extinction. With the loving support from the community and people who love what they are about, they firmly believe they can truly make a difference.
